* Intro
+ must be enabled in bash: =shopt -s extglob=
+ not part of POSIX
+ power similar to that of EREs

* Extended notations

+ =*(exp)=
  - 0+ of /exp/
  - equivalent to ERE =(exp*)=
+ =+(exp)=
  - 1+ of /exp/
  - equivalent to ERE =(exp+)=
+ =?(exp)=
  - 0|1 of /exp/
  - equivalent to ERE =(exp?)=
+ =@(exp1|exp2|...)=
  - alternation (one of)
  - equivalent to ERE =exp1|exp2|...=
+ =!(exp1)=
  - complement of /exp/
  - no equivalent

* Use cases
+ more powerful filename matching

#+begin_src bash :results output verbatim
shopt -s extglob
ls @(in*|*ss*)
